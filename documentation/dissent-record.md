# Dissent Record — Ontology Documentation

## Definition

A formal scientific disagreement with a finding.

## Purpose

Preserves scientific controversy and alternative interpretations.

## Fields

See `entities/dissent-record/dissent-record.documentation.md`.

## Relationships

- CONTRASTS_WITH → Finding
- CITES → Citation
- SUPPORTED_BY → Evidence

## Examples

- A registered disagreement about the magnitude of urban heat island effects on global temperature trends.

## Non-Examples

- A casual comment.
- A duplicate of the original finding.

## Validation Rules

- Statement, confidence, citations, evidence, and status are mandatory.

## Version Rules

- Mandatory versioning on statement or status change.

## Governance Rules

- Submitted by authenticated scientific users.
- Reviewed by Scientific Reviewer within 30 days.

## Future Evolution Rules

- Threaded discussion and AI-assisted conflict detection.
