## 1. Confidence Terms

A qualitative expert judgement of the validity of a finding, calibrated to IPCC-style language.

| Level | Label | Numeric Anchor | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `VeryLow` | 0.0–0.2 | Limited evidence, low agreement, high uncertainty. |
| 2 | `Low` | 0.2–0.4 | Limited evidence or low agreement. |
| 3 | `Medium` | 0.4–0.6 | Medium evidence and agreement. |
| 4 | `High` | 0.6–0.8 | Robust evidence and high agreement. |
| 5 | `VeryHigh` | 0.8–1.0 | Comprehensive evidence and high agreement. |

---

## 2. Likelihood Terms

A probabilistic judgement of an outcome or result, calibrated to IPCC likelihood language.

| Level | Label | Probability Range |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `ExceptionallyUnlikely` | 0–1% |
| 2 | `VeryUnlikely` | 0–10% |
| 3 | `Unlikely` | 0–33% |
| 4 | `AboutAsLikelyAsNot` | 33–66% |
| 5 | `Likely` | 33–100% (commonly 66–100%) |
| 6 | `VeryLikely` | 66–100% (commonly 90–100%) |
| 7 | `VirtuallyCertain` | 99–100% |

*Note: For strict semantic modeling, `Likely` = 66–100%, `VeryLikely` = 90–100%.*

## 3. Observation Types

| Type | Definition |
|---|---|
| `InSitu` | Direct measurement at a station or field location. |
| `RemoteSensing` | Satellite, aircraft, or drone observation. |
| `Proxy` | Indirect reconstruction from paleoclimate archives. |
| `Reanalysis` | Model-data fusion product. |
| `Survey` | Structured sampling or census. |

## 4. Evidence Types

| Type | Definition |
|---|---|
| `Observation` | Empirical measurement. |
| `Experiment` | Controlled laboratory or field experiment. |
| `Model` | Numerical or conceptual model result. |
| `Assessment` | Expert synthesis of multiple lines of evidence. |
| `MetaAnalysis` | Statistical synthesis of multiple studies. |
| `Dataset` | Structured data collection. |
| `Simulation` | Controlled numerical experiment. |

| Category | Examples |
